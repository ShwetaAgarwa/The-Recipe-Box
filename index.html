Appetizers

Vegetable Spring Rolls (Healthy & Vegan)

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, julienned
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 8 rice paper wrappers
  • Fresh cilantro and mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce (low sodium)
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Instructions:

  1. Soak the rice paper wrappers in warm water for 20 seconds until soft.
  2. Place the rice paper on a flat surface and layer the vegetables, avocado, and herbs in the center.
  3. Fold in the sides and roll tightly.
  4. Mix soy sauce, rice vinegar, and sesame oil for the dipping sauce.
  5. Serve the rolls with the dipping sauce.

Chickpea and Avocado Salad Toast

Ingredients:

  • 1 can chickpeas (drained and rinsed)
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup cucumber, diced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Whole wheat baguette slices, toasted

Instructions:

  1. Mash the chickpeas in a bowl until chunky.
  2. Add onion, cucumber, olive oil, lemon juice, dill, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Spoon the chickpea mixture onto the toasted baguette slices.
  4. Serve as an appetizer or snack.

your healthy Toast is ready!! Enjoyy...

Moong Dal Halwa

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Moong Dal (yellow split lentils)
  • 1 cup Milk (low-fat or almond milk for a healthier version)
  • 2 tbsp Ghee
  • 1 tbsp Jaggery (or honey for a lighter option)
  • 1/4 tsp Cardamom Powder
  • 10-12 Cashews and Almonds (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup Water

Instructions:

  1. Sabse pehle, moong dal ko achhe se dhoke, 2-3 ghante ke liye bhigo dena hai.
  2. Phir, dal ko coarsely grind kar lo.
  3. Ek pan mein ghee garam karo, usme moong dal daal kar halki aanch par bhuno jab tak wo thodi si golden na ho jaye.
  4. Ab ismein thoda paani daal kar dal ko cook karo.
  5. Jab dal achhe se cook ho jaye, to usmein milk daal kar cook karte raho jab tak wo creamy consistency mein na aa jaye.
  6. Ismein jaggery ya honey aur cardamom powder daal kar mix karo.
  7. Lastly, dry fruits daal kar mix karo aur thodi der aur cook karo
  8. Serve karte waqt garam-garam halwa ka maza lo!

Ragi Laddoo

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Ragi Flour (finger millet flour)
  • 1/4 cup Jaggery (grated)
  • 1/4 cup Ghee
  • 1/4 tsp Cardamom Powder

  • 1 tbsp Desiccated Coconut (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Ek pan mein ghee garam karo aur usmein ragi flour daal kar bhunein jab tak wo halka golden na ho jaye.
  2. Ab jaggery daal kar usse melt karne dein.
  3. Cardamom powder aur chopped nuts daal kar mix karein.
  4. Jab mixture thoda thanda ho jaye, to usse small laddoos mein shape dekar roll kar lein.
  5. Agar chahiye to desiccated coconut se coat bhi kar sakte hain.
  6. Ragi laddoos ready hain! Serve karte waqt enjoy karein

Iced Matcha Latte (Refreshing and Energizing)

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp Matcha Powder
  • 1/2 cup Hot Water
  • 1/2 cup Milk (or any milk substitute)
  • 1-2 tsp Honey or Sweetener
  • Ice Cubes

Instructions:

  1. Matcha powder ko garam paani mein dissolve kar lein.
  2. Milk ko thoda garam karen ya chilled rakh lein (apni preference ke hisaab se).
  3. Ek glass mein ice cubes daal kar, matcha mixture aur milk daal ke mix karein.
  4. Sweeten with honey or your preferred sweetener.
  5. Serve and enjoy this energizing drink on a hot day!

Thandai(Traditional Indian Drink)

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1/4 cup Almonds (soaked overnight)

  • 1 tbsp Fennel Seeds
  • 1/2 tbsp Peppercorns
  • 1-2 tbsp Sugar or Honey
  • 1/4 tsp Rose Water (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp Cardamom Powder

Instructions:

  1. Sab dry ingredients (almonds, pistachios, fennel seeds, peppercorns) ko grinder mein daal kar coarsely grind kar lein.
  2. Milk ko garam karen, phir ground mixture aur cardamom powder daal kar mix kar lein.
  3. Sugar ya honey daal kar stir karein.
  4. Rose water add kar sakte hain for an aromatic touch.
  5. Serve chilled, perfect for festivals or gatherings!
